Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book Omega?

We recommend booking at least 4–8 weeks in advance for most events. For large productions, festivals, or international engagements, 3–6 months is preferred to ensure availability and preparation time.

Are the singing lessons really free?

Yes. Omega's core lessons and warmup routines are available at no cost. Her mission is to make great vocal training accessible to everyone, regardless of budget or experience level.

Does Omega perform internationally?

Absolutely. Omega has performed worldwide throughout her career and welcomes international bookings. Travel and accommodation arrangements can be discussed as part of the booking process.

Can Omega customise her setlist for my event?

Yes — every performance is tailored. During the consultation process, Omega works with you to understand your audience, event tone, and any specific musical requests to create the perfect set.

I'm a complete beginner. Are the lessons right for me?

The lessons are designed for everyone — from singers who have never held a note to working professionals looking to refine their craft. Omega's curriculum meets you exactly where you are.

What genres does Omega perform?

Jazz, Musical Theater, Gospel, Soul, and Classical. Each genre is part of Omega's professional repertoire — not a style she approximates, but a world she genuinely inhabits.
