The Artist

A Voice That Has Traveled the World

Omega Bone is a Los Angeles-based vocalist whose extraordinary range spans Jazz, Musical Theater, Gospel, Soul, and Classical music. With a career that has taken her to stages around the globe, she brings uncommon depth and fire to every performance.

Beyond the stage, Omega is deeply committed to passing her gifts forward — training singers, musicians, and professionals in a supportive, transformative environment.
