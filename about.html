About Omega

A dynamic artist whose supercharged vocals bring music to life and crowds to their feet — Omega Bone has traveled the world, delivering dazzling performances across Jazz, Musical Theater, Gospel, Soul, and Classical music.

Omega Bone, Los Angeles

The Story

Born to Move People

Omega Bone grew up with music running through everything. From early performances in gospel choirs to rigorous classical training, she developed a voice that refuses to be confined to a single genre — or a single continent.

Based in Los Angeles, Omega has performed at venues and events across the world, bringing her extraordinary vocal range and magnetic stage presence to jazz clubs, concert halls, theatrical stages, and intimate private gatherings alike.

The Artist

Five Genres, One Voice

What sets Omega apart isn't just her technical mastery — it's her emotional intelligence. She inhabits every genre she touches: the spontaneity of Jazz, the devotion of Gospel, the narrative depth of Musical Theater, the rawness of Soul, and the precision of Classical music.

Her credits span live performance, recording, and video game music (credited on multiple titles via VGMdb) — a testament to the versatility that producers, directors, and event planners keep coming back for.

The Educator

Training Voices, Changing Lives

Omega's teaching philosophy is built on consistency, patience, and deep respect for every student's unique voice. She has trained singers from complete beginners to working professionals, building a curriculum of 48+ exercises available in all 12 keys.

Her 10-minute daily warmup routine has been praised by students across the world for its simplicity and transformative results — proof that small, consistent practice unlocks extraordinary potential.
